What is VLCTechFest?

VLCTechFest brings together software development professionals to share practices and experiences and thus improve their skills.

Our objectives are clear:

  • Share good practices and trends in professional software development.
  • Connect the different actors in the software development industry in Valencia.
  • Involve collectives and individuals in the organization and programming of the conference.

Call for papers
